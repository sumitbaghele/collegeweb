Posted on May 20, 2025

A three-day Workshop on Web Development was successfully conducted by the Department of Computer Science at DB Science College, Gondia from May 17 to May 19, 2025.

📚 Workshop Overview:

  • HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript basics
  • Responsive design and Bootstrap introduction
  • Live coding sessions and student hands-on practice
  • GitHub basics and project hosting

👨‍🏫 Resource Persons:

  • Mr. Rajesh Korde (Frontend Developer, Nagpur)
  • Ms. Sneha Patil (Full Stack Trainer)

🎯 Target Audience:

Final year BCA and B.Sc (Computer Science) students

🏆 Outcome:

Over 60 students participated and built mini projects. E-certificates were provided to all attendees.

📸 Photos & Feedback

Photos, student feedback, and recorded sessions will be shared soon via the official college portal.

📢 HOD’s Message

"This workshop aimed to bridge the gap between academics and industry expectations. We’re proud of our students for their active participation," said HOD Mr. Ajay Deshmukh.
