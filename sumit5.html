NCC Parade & Training Camp

Posted on March 25, 2025

The NCC unit of DB Science College, Gondia successfully organized a Parade & Training Camp from March 20 to March 24, 2025. The camp focused on discipline, fitness, and national pride through structured activities and training sessions.

🪖 Key Highlights:

  • Daily early morning parade and drill practice
  • Fitness and obstacle training sessions
  • Lectures on leadership, discipline, and civic duty
  • Patriotism-based cultural events and group activities

🎓 Participants:

Over 60 NCC cadets from various streams participated under the guidance of CTO Prof. Vaibhav Kadu and senior NCC officers.

🎤 Guest Speakers:

  • Lt. Col. Sunil Raut – “Discipline & National Service”
  • Dr. Archana Mohod – “Physical and Mental Health for Cadets”

📸 Camp Gallery:

Photos and highlights from the camp will be uploaded soon to the college NCC page and gallery.

📢 NCC Officer’s Message:

“The training instilled a strong sense of responsibility and teamwork. We are proud of our cadets’ performance and discipline,” said CTO Prof. Vaibhav Kadu.

Unity and discipline – the motto of NCC lives on! 🇮🇳
