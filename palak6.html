Department Of Zoology

In the department of zoology, the study of all animals take place. Including aquatic animals, marine animals, herbivores animals carnivores animals, omnivores animals, etc. In this department, we study all the species of animals and kinds of animals. Here we take the knowledge of animals that how they survive in the envioronment.

Courses Offered

The Zoology Department typically offers the following academic programs:B.Sc. in Zoology (Bachelor of Science), M.Sc. in Zoology (Master of Science),M.Phil. and Ph.D. in Zoology (for research).

Interdisciplinary courses

Some colleges may also offer honors, minor courses, or interdisciplinary courses with Environmental Science, Biotechnology, or Microbiology.

Department Activities

Lab Experiments: Dissections, microscopy, and biochemical tests. Field Visits: Zoos, wildlife sanctuaries, national parks, or marine ecosystems.
