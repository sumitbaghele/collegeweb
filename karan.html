Intermediate

Intermediate level refer to the education after high school (10th grade) and before graduation. It Includes course for students of 11th and 12th classes. These courses build a strong foundation in subjects based on their chosen stream.

Graduate

Graduation is the first level of higher education pursued after completing 12th standard. It usually spans 3 to 4 years depending on the course and stream, and provides in -depth academic and professional knowledge in a chosen field.

Post-Graduate

Post-graduate is and advanced level of education pursued after completing a graduation degree . These course help students specialize in a particular subject or profession and are essenbtial for research, teaching, or high-level careers.
